Help: Provide Research Context and Upload Your Data

In this screen, you're asked to upload the core materials related to your research. This includes your dataset (as a ZIP file), and any contextual documents that help explain it - such as research papers, abstracts, README files, or metadata.

You'll also be asked to specify the domain of your research. You can either select from the provided list or describe it freely in your own words. Additionally, there is a space to enter any extra information that may not be captured in your files - details that you consider important for understanding your dataset or its background.

The goal of this step is to give the system as much context as possible. This information will be used in later stages to identify relevant concepts, extract entities and relationships, and build a meaningful knowledge graph.

The more detail you provide here, the more accurate and useful the resulting graph will be.
