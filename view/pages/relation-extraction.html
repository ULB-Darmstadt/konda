Help: Define and Review Relationships Between Concepts

In this step, the app has analyzed your dataset and documents to identify possible relationships between the key terms (named entities and categories) from the previous screen. These relationships are written as simple statements, each made up of three parts:

  • Subject: an entity or category
  • Verb: the relation between them
  • Object: another entity or category

These statements describe how things in your research are connected - such as what is used, produced, measured, or related to what.

Your task here is to:

  • Check whether the suggested relationships make sense
  • Edit or correct any parts of them (all fields are editable)
  • Remove entries that are incorrect or irrelevant
  • Add new relationships if something important is missing

You can choose the subject and object from the named entities and categories identified earlier. The relation (verb) is free text, but for already generated relations the app offers suggestions to help guide you.

Getting these relationships right will allow the app to build a richer and more useful knowledge graph to support more complex questions and provide deeper insights into your dataset.
